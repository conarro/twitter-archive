Portability:

Often difficult by design (vendor lock-in), but sometimes an accidental byproduct.

When building, explore how your abstractions and data might be translated into standard, portable formats.

This can produce valuable insights as you shape out primitives.

Constraining power:

Abstracting away cumbersome, technical stuff is part of the game. But consider the "lossiness" of your abstractions. Can your abstracted form be translated to a standard without losing information? Can a standard be *imported*?
